Topic-finding workshop
1. Dump all ideas
2. Gather feedback
3. Pick one
Dump every idea. Post-it is great. Anything. Don't judge. Tell it to ther, hear their feedback. What they would like to hear, or not. Try to sort, pick the one that appeals the most to you and focus on that one.

Where can I do a talk?
"I don't go to meetups, I know no one"
A successful talk is...
  • ⅓ content
  • ⅓ speaker
  • ⅓ audience
You can have the most interesting content and be an amazing speaker, if people don' care it's wasted. Adapt your content to your audience.
